# Selected Career Case Studies

These anonymized examples illustrate the types of business challenges I have worked on and how I approach growth. They intentionally exclude confidential customer and employer information.

## Case Study 1: PropTech Enterprise Growth

### Situation

A technology organization needed to accelerate enterprise growth in a complex market with long sales cycles and multiple stakeholders.

### Approach

I focused on deeper discovery, structured qualification, strategic account mapping, executive engagement, and identifying both new-logo and expansion opportunities. Conversations were connected to business outcomes rather than feature-level presentations.

### Outcome

The approach contributed to multimillion-dollar contract value growth and performance above annual sales targets.

### Capabilities Demonstrated

Complex deal strategy • Consultative selling • Stakeholder management • Qualification discipline • Account growth

---

## Case Study 2: BFSI Account Expansion

### Situation

Strategic financial-services relationships required stronger retention, executive engagement, and a more systematic approach to identifying growth opportunities.

### Approach

I used structured customer engagement, business reviews, cross-functional collaboration, and consultative conversations to better understand changing priorities and identify relevant expansion opportunities.

### Outcome

The work supported improved retention and additional revenue through targeted upsell and cross-sell opportunities.

### Capabilities Demonstrated

Strategic account management • Executive relationships • Customer growth • Cross-functional collaboration • Financial-services experience

---

## Case Study 3: Strategic Partner Development

### Situation

Growth required stronger relationships with complementary technology and services organizations.

### Approach

I evaluated strategic fit, developed joint value propositions, mapped target accounts, aligned internal and partner stakeholders, and supported collaborative GTM motions.

### Outcome

The work expanded ecosystem relationships and contributed to partner-influenced pipeline and market opportunities.

### Capabilities Demonstrated

Alliance strategy • Partner development • Joint GTM • Account mapping • Ecosystem-led growth

---

## Closing Perspective

Across each example, the common thread is a focus on understanding the business problem, aligning the right stakeholders, creating measurable value, and building relationships that support sustainable growth.
