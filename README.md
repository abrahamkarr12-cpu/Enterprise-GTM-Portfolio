## How I Approach Growth

Complex enterprise deals are rarely won by delivering the best product presentation. They are won by understanding the business, the people involved, the cost of inaction, and how the organization will make a decision.

My process starts with five questions:

1. What problem is the organization actually trying to solve?
2. Why does it matter now?
3. Who is affected, and who influences the decision?
4. What happens if nothing changes?
5. How will success be measured?

From there, I work backward to understand stakeholders, decision criteria, business impact, competition, internal processes, and the path to a decision.
